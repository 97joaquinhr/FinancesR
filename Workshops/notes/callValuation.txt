Example for call valuation using multi-step binomial pricing
Select any stock
S0=Stock price today
	- Download weekly price history for at least 1 year
	- Calculate returns (cc)
	- Calculate volatility of returns (std. dev.)
	- Calculate the parameters u and d as:
		u=(1+Ru)	d=(1-Rd)
			u=e^(volatility)

	e^r=growth rate=(1+R)
	e^sdret~=(1+sdret)
